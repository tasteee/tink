z-pattern-roll

A chord-relative pattern editor. Rows are chord degrees — N1 is the root of the active chord, N2 the 2nd chord tone, and so on. Author the shape once; it plays over any chord. Draw mode click-drags a signal; Select mode marquees / double-click places. Drag to move, edge to resize, overlap trims (latest wins). Keys: Del · Ctrl/⌘+D dup · Shift+↑↓ octave · Alt+↑↓ velocity · ,/. probability · M mute · R reset.
